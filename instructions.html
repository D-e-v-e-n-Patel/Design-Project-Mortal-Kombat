About


Mortal Kombat is an American fighting video game brand that began with the release of the same-named game in 1992. Mortal Kombat began as a video game series, but has since grown into a multi-platform media phenomenon that encompasses films, animated content, toys, and other merchandising.

How to Play

Abbreviation key for attacks and commands in all character guides:
F = Forward
B = Back
D = Down
U = Up
LP = Low Punch
LK = Low Kick
HP = High Punch
HK = High Kick
BL = Block
JK = Jump Kick
RH = Roundhouse
